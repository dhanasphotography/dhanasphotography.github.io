Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to some of the most common questions from our clients.

01
What is included in a pre-wedding shoot?

Our pre-wedding shoot includes location planning, candid photos, cinematic short video, professional editing, and outfit guidance. You’ll receive high-res digital images and a teaser video.

02
Do you provide candid wedding photography?

Yes, we specialize in candid wedding photography that captures natural moments and emotions without staged poses. This style gives your album an authentic, storytelling feel.

03
How long does it take to deliver photos/videos?

We typically deliver edited photos within 10–15 working days and videos within 20 days. Urgent delivery options are available upon request.

04
Can we choose our own shoot location?

Absolutely! You can suggest your preferred locations for outdoor shoots. We also provide recommendations based on your theme and budget.

05
What types of events do you cover?

We cover weddings, engagements, birthdays, corporate events, maternity shoots, baby showers, and more. Our team can tailor coverage for any special occasion.

06
Do you offer drone coverage or live streaming?

Yes, we offer drone photography and 4K aerial video coverage as add-ons. Live streaming options are also available for weddings and events.
